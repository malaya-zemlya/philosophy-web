To assert that [libertarian agent-causation](../concepts/agent-causation.md) is genuinely metaphysically
possible on a priori grounds is to assert the ideal conceivability of a scenario — agents
originating action against the background of the complete causal facts — and ideal conceivability
just is consistency under ideal a priori reasoning
([ideal-conceivability-is-apriori-consistency](ideal-conceivability-is-apriori-consistency.md)). Certifying the consistency of a scenario
that rich is beyond any effective procedure
([consistency-certification-is-oracle-hard](consistency-certification-is-oracle-hard.md)). So the libertarian's modal credential, when
it rests on conceivability, is an uncertified Π₁ coherence claim: "no contradiction found so far"
is all a computable reasoner can honestly report, and that prima facie notion is not what the
possibility claim needs.

The claim is the free-will twin of [zombie-premise-unwarrantable](zombie-premise-unwarrantable.md), produced by the one
dictionary entry of the [consciousness–free-will duality](../concepts/consciousness-freewill-duality.md)
that transfers *machinery* rather than analogy — the Π₁ certificate objection applies verbatim on
both sides of the mirror ([agent-causation-certificate](../arguments/agent-causation-certificate.md)). Its scope matches its twin's:
it concerns warrant, not truth — agent-causation may yet be possible; what fails is the armchair
entitlement to assert so. It does not touch inference-rule arguments for incompatibilism such as
van Inwagen's Consequence Argument ([van-inwagen-1983](../sources/van-inwagen-1983.md)), which carry different failure
modes.

### In plain terms

Defenders of a strong "uncaused chooser" picture of free will often say: it's clearly *coherent*,
so it's at least possible. This claim says nobody is entitled to the word "clearly." Certifying
that a scenario as rich as real agency-plus-physics hides no contradiction *anywhere* is provably
harder than any step-by-step reasoning can achieve — the same trap that catches the famous
[zombie argument](zombie-premise-unwarrantable.md) on the consciousness side. The honest report
is "no contradiction found *yet*," and that proves nothing.
