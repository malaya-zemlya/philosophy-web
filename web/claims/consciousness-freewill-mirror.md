The two problem-families are structurally the same debate run in opposite directions: positions,
objections, and argument-shapes on the consciousness side map systematically onto counterparts on
the free-will side under reversal of the perception–action axis, and the mirroring is regular
enough to *predict* transferable moves before they are found
([consciousness-freewill-duality](../concepts/consciousness-freewill-duality.md)).

This is the hedged master thesis of the duality framework — deliberately weaker than
[consciousness-freewill-strict-duality](consciousness-freewill-strict-duality.md), which asserts a categorical duality outright. The
mirror thesis commits only to a systematic correspondence of dialectics: a further-fact camp and a
deflationary camp on each side, mirrored objections (exclusion and the
[disappearing agent](../arguments/disappearing-agent.md)), mirrored
intuition pumps (zombies and volitional zombies), mirrored unity problems, and at least one
verbatim transfer of formal machinery. It would be falsified if the dictionary's entries proved
cherry-picked — if systematic translation attempts mostly landed on nothing. Established
abductively by [consciousness-freewill-duality](../arguments/consciousness-freewill-duality.md); who denies it: anyone for whom the
correspondences reflect only the generic shape of realist-versus-deflationist disputes, common to
any philosophical stalemate, rather than a mirror specific to the perception–action axis.
