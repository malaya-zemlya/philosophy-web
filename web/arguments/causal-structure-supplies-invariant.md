inference form: deductive — from a constraint on implementation plus its objectivity to the existence
of the demanded invariant.

1. ([implementation-requires-counterfactual-structure](../claims/implementation-requires-counterfactual-structure.md)) Genuine implementation needs robust
   counterfactual dispositions across the whole transition table, excluding gerrymandered carvings
   (Chalmers vs Putnam, 1996).
2. ([counterfactual-structure-objective](../claims/counterfactual-structure-objective.md)) Those counterfactual-causal facts are objective,
   not observer-relative.
∴ ([causal-structure-fixes-decomposition](../claims/causal-structure-fixes-decomposition.md)) The admissible decomposition is fixed by objective
   causal structure — so the invariant [decomposition-indeterminacy](decomposition-indeterminacy.md) demands already
   exists, and it is the organisational grain ([role-grain-is-organisational](../claims/role-grain-is-organisational.md),
   [grain](../concepts/grain.md)). This `attacks` that argument at its premise 1
   ([functional-decomposition-relative](../claims/functional-decomposition-relative.md)).

bearing: answers the indeterminacy objection on the functionalist's behalf and ties the demanded
invariant to the organisational-grain view already in the web.

weakest premise: the step from (1)+(2) to (3) overreaches. The anti-triviality result secures
*non-triviality*, not *uniqueness*; and the (interventionist) causal facts are objective only relative
to a chosen variable set. So the conclusion is contested by
[counterfactuals-yield-non-triviality-not-uniqueness](counterfactuals-yield-non-triviality-not-uniqueness.md) (a residual family of carvings
survives) and by [relevance-filter-regress](relevance-filter-regress.md) (causal structure alone does not select the
*mind-relevant* relations — the squishy-noise problem).

Distinct from [role-grain-is-organisational](../claims/role-grain-is-organisational.md), which it supports: that fixes *which* grain is
right; this argues that *a* grain is fixed at all.
