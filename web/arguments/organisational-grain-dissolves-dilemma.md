The *organisational-grain middle* is a rebuttal to [the grain
dilemma](role-grain-dilemma.md). That dilemma succeeds only if the ways of specifying a functional role are exhausted by
"coarse input–output" and "biological microstructure," leaving no middle ground. This argument
denies that by supplying the middle, so the dilemma's exhaustiveness premise fails and
[substrate independence](../claims/substrate-independence.md) is left standing.

1. The mind-individuating role is fixed at the grain of internal counterfactual-causal
   organisation — the state-to-state transition structure among a system's components
   ([organisational role-grain](../claims/role-grain-is-organisational.md)). This is a third option,
   distinct from both gross input–output and physical-realiser microchemistry.
2. *It escapes horn 1 (too liberal / the blockhead).* A [lookup table](../concepts/lookup-table-mind.md)
   and the homunculi-headed [Chinese Nation](../concepts/chinese-nation.md) realise the input–output
   profile precisely *by lacking* the internal transition structure: the lookup table is a flat
   query→answer map with no mediating states in the right counterfactual web, and the China-brain
   mimics gross behaviour without the dense intra-system dependencies. They fail the organisational
   grain, excluded by a fact about *causal topology*, not about substrate.
3. *It escapes horn 2 (too chauvinistic / collapse toward proteocentrism).* Transition structure
   is multiply realisable: silicon that reproduces it realises the role, and a molecular-twin brain
   is sufficient but not necessary. Carbon does no individuative work, so there is no collapse
   toward [proteocentrism](../positions/proteocentrism.md).
4. *The line is principled, not gerrymandered for the conclusion.* It is independently the grain at
   which the explanatory and predictive generalisations of psychology and computational
   neuroscience quantify — gross input–output is too coarse to distinguish a calculator from an
   arithmetician, while carbon chemistry is too fine to figure in any cognitive generalisation. The
   scientifically load-bearing grain and the proposed grain coincide, and that coincidence is a
   reason to draw the line here that owes nothing to wanting silicon to qualify.

Therefore the dilemma's exhaustiveness premise is false: substrate independence has a grain coarse
enough for silicon, fine enough to exclude the blockhead, and motivated independently of the verdict
it delivers. This is the principled intermediate grain the grain dilemma demanded.

### Weakest premise

Premise 2 — whether the blockhead *really* fails the organisational grain, or whether a
sufficiently elaborated lookup or China system can be redescribed as instantiating the requisite
transition structure after all. If it can, horn 1 reopens. This is precisely the opening pressed by
[the dense China-brain regress](dense-china-brain-regress.md).

Distinct from [the phenomenal-concepts reply](gap-deflated-by-phenomenal-concepts.md), which
addresses the phenomenal residue (what role leaves out): this argument addresses the individuation
of role itself, and applies even to intentional states.

### In plain terms

This answers the dilemma that says no good level of detail exists ([role-grain-dilemma](role-grain-dilemma.md))
by offering one. That trap: describe a mind too loosely and a mere cheat-sheet machine qualifies;
describe it too tightly and only biological brains qualify. The middle option proposed here:
describe a mind by its *inner wiring of cause-and-effect* — which inner states trigger which others,
and how — rather than by its outward behavior or by its exact biology. That middle does both jobs at
once. It shuts out the cheat-sheet, which has no such inner wiring (it just maps questions straight
to stored answers). And it lets in silicon, because the same wiring pattern can be rebuilt in chips:
the carbon was never doing the real work. And it isn't rigged to favor machines — it just happens to
be the very level brain science and psychology already use to explain how minds work. The soft spot:
maybe a clever enough cheat-sheet-style system *could* be re-described as having the right inner
wiring after all, and if so the cheat-sheet sneaks back in.
