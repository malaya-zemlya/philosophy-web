conscious requires only that its content be poised/available to a higher-order consumer system, not

cited in [consciousness-is-higher-order-representation](../claims/consciousness-is-higher-order-representation.md), [higher-order-theories](../positions/higher-order-theories.md),
and [order-of-representation](../concepts/order-of-representation.md).
