relevance (content): the rule-following considerations (roughly §§138–242) and the private-language
argument, on which understanding a rule is not the grasping of an item that determines all its

[kripke-1982](kripke-1982.md); cited in [kripke-rule-following](../arguments/kripke-rule-following.md).
