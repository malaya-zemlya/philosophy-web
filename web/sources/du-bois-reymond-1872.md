relevance (content): the lecture that gave the consciousness problem its famous slogan *ignorabimus*
("we shall not know") — Du Bois-Reymond argued that how matter gives rise to consciousness is among
the questions natural science can never answer. Cited in
[hard-problem-conceived-de-novo-by-humans](../claims/hard-problem-conceived-de-novo-by-humans.md) as a node in the human lineage of hard-problem
thinking.

Reference:
- Du Bois-Reymond, E. (1872). *Über die Grenzen des Naturerkennens* [On the limits of our knowledge
  of nature]. Veit & Comp.
