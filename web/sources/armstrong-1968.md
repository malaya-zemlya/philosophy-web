relevance (content): the standard reference for the demand that a disposition requires a *categorical
basis* — a "would-do" property holds in virtue of some actual, occurrent state of the thing. Grounds the
categorical/dispositional contrast invoked by [consciousness-categorical-not-dispositional](../claims/consciousness-categorical-not-dispositional.md).

Reference:
- Armstrong, D. M. (1968). *A materialist theory of the mind*. Routledge & Kegan Paul.
