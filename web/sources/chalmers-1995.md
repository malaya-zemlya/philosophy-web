The paper that coined "the hard problem of consciousness" and drew the easy/hard contrast that
organizes the consciousness sub-web. Grounds [hard-problem](../concepts/hard-problem.md) and the open
[hard-problem](../questions/hard-problem.md). Distinct from [chalmers-1996](chalmers-1996.md), the later book *The Conscious
Mind*, which grounds the zombie and conceivability apparatus.

Reference:
- Chalmers, D. J. (1995). Facing up to the problem of consciousness. *Journal of Consciousness
  Studies, 2*(3), 200–219.
