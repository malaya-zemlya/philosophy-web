relevance (content): Pamela McCorduck's history of artificial intelligence, source of the classic
formulation of the **[AI effect](../concepts/ai-effect.md)** — her observation that "it's part of the history of
the field of artificial intelligence that every time somebody figured out how to make a computer do
something … there was a chorus of critics to say, 'that's not thinking.'" Grounds [ai-effect](../concepts/ai-effect.md).

Reference:
- McCorduck, P. (1979). *Machines who think: A personal inquiry into the history and prospects of artificial intelligence*. W. H. Freeman. (2nd ed., A K Peters, 2004)
