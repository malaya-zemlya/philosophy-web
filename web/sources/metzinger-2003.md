relevance (content): the self-model theory of subjectivity, and in particular *phenomenal
transparency* — the representing vehicle is invisible to the system; only the represented content
shows up in experience. Cited in [llm-access-similarity-not-encoding](../claims/llm-access-similarity-not-encoding.md) as the precedent for a
system having access to the content/structure of its states but not to the encoding that carries
them.

Reference:
- Metzinger, T. (2003). *Being no one: The self-model theory of subjectivity*. MIT Press.
