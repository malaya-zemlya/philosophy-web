relevance (content): the coinage of the term *qualia* in its modern sense. Clarence Irving Lewis
introduces "qualia" for the recognisable qualitative characters of the given that recur across
different experiences. Primary provenance for [qualia](../concepts/qualia.md). (Distinct from David Lewis, the
author of [lewis-1988](lewis-1988.md).)

Reference:
- Lewis, C. I. (1929). *Mind and the world-order: Outline of a theory of knowledge*. Charles
  Scribner's Sons.
