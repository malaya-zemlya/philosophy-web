relevance (content): Carnap's distinction between *internal* questions (answerable within a linguistic
framework) and *external* questions about whether to adopt the framework, the latter to be settled not
by correspondence but by *fruitfulness* — the efficiency and utility of the framework for its purposes.
The fruitfulness test cited in [unobservables-judged-by-coherence-and-usefulness](../claims/unobservables-judged-by-coherence-and-usefulness.md) and
[unobservability-in-principle](../concepts/unobservability-in-principle.md).

Reference:
- Carnap, R. (1950). Empiricism, semantics, and ontology. *Revue Internationale de Philosophie, 4*(11),
  20–40.
