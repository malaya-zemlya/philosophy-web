relevance (content): states *conscious realism* and the *interface theory of perception* — the
objective world consists of conscious agents and their experiences, while spacetime and physical
objects are a species-specific perceptual interface rather than fundamental reality. Grounds the
conscious-realism variant of [idealism](../positions/idealism.md).

Reference:
- Hoffman, D. D. (2008). Conscious realism and the mind-body problem. *Mind and Matter, 6*(1),
  87–121.
