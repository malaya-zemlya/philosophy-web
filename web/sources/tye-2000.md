relevance (content): one source of the **collapse objection** against the indexical reply (with
Chalmers): Mary suffers no ordinary self-locating ignorance, so any residual gap must be carried by a
demonstrative *phenomenal* concept of the experience-type, collapsing the indexical move into the
phenomenal-concept strategy. Tye holds colour-blind Mary lacks certain phenomenal concepts and that
this leaves naturalism untouched. Cited at the weakest-premise objection in [indexical-reply](../arguments/indexical-reply.md).

Reference:
- Tye, M. (2000). *Consciousness, color, and content*. MIT Press.
