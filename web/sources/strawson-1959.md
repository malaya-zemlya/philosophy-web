relevance (content): P. F. Strawson's *Individuals*, the work that revived the
[transcendental argument](../concepts/transcendental-argument.md) in analytic philosophy as "descriptive
metaphysics" — reasoning from the necessary conditions of our conceptual scheme (for instance, what must
hold for us to identify and reidentify particulars at all) to substantive conclusions about the world and
the self. Grounds [transcendental-argument](../concepts/transcendental-argument.md).

Reference:
- Strawson, P. F. (1959). *Individuals: An essay in descriptive metaphysics*. Methuen.
