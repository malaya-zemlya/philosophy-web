relevance (content): the seminal statement of mysterianism — there exists a natural property of the
brain that explains how it gives rise to consciousness, but we are *cognitively closed* to it.
Grounds the naturalist premise in [consciousness-has-natural-explanation](../claims/consciousness-has-natural-explanation.md) (and the closure
half in [humans-closed-to-psychophysical-link](../claims/humans-closed-to-psychophysical-link.md)).

Reference:
- McGinn, C. (1989). Can we solve the mind–body problem? *Mind, 98*(391), 349–366.
  https://doi.org/10.1093/mind/XCVIII.391.349
