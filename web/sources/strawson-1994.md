The *Basic Argument* against ultimate [sourcehood](../concepts/sourcehood.md): to be truly the source of one's
actions one would have to be the source of the will they issue from, and of the will that formed
*that* will, and so on — one would have to be *causa sui*, which is impossible. The regress is
Strawson's, and it presses the source condition from the same direction whether or not determinism
holds.

Reference: Strawson, G. (1994). The impossibility of moral responsibility. *Philosophical Studies*, *75*(1–2), 5–24. https://doi.org/10.1007/BF00989879
