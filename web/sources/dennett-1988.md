relevance (content): Dennett's argument that nothing answers to qualia as traditionally conceived
(intrinsic, ineffable, private, and directly apprehended), so the thick notion should be abandoned,
or "quined." The critical foil for [qualia](../concepts/qualia.md).

Reference:
- Dennett, D. C. (1988). Quining qualia. In A. J. Marcel & E. Bisiach (Eds.), *Consciousness in
  contemporary science* (pp. 42–77). Oxford University Press.
