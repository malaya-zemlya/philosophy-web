relevance (content): Daniel Dennett's collected statement of the *intentional stance* — the
strategy of predicting a system's behaviour by treating it as a rational agent with beliefs and
desires — and of the interpretivist thesis that being a true believer just is being a system whose
behaviour is reliably predictable from that stance. Explicitly denies any principled line between
*original* and *derived* intentionality: our own aboutness is derived too, from the design work of
evolution. Grounds [intentional-stance](../positions/intentional-stance.md) and [intentionality-is-stance-relative](../claims/intentionality-is-stance-relative.md).

Reference:
- Dennett, D. C. (1987). *The intentional stance*. MIT Press.
