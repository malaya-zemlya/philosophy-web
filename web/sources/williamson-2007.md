relevance (content): Williamson's account of philosophical methodology and the epistemology of thought
experiments, on which "intuitions" are ordinary judgments applying a concept to a described case rather
than a special faculty, and their evidential standing depends on our reliability about cases of that
kind. Background for [intuition-warrant-tracks-trained-domain](../claims/intuition-warrant-tracks-trained-domain.md).

Reference:
- Williamson, T. (2007). *The philosophy of philosophy*. Blackwell.
