relevance (content): Dennett's treatment of *intuition pumps* — thought experiments engineered to
elicit a verdict, whose persuasive force can hinge on incidental "knobs" rather than the point at
issue, so the elicited intuition warrants scrutiny. Cited in
[intuition-warrant-tracks-trained-domain](../claims/intuition-warrant-tracks-trained-domain.md). (Dennett coined "intuition pump" earlier, in *Elbow
Room*, 1984; this is the dedicated treatment.)

Reference:
- Dennett, D. C. (2013). *Intuition pumps and other tools for thinking*. W. W. Norton & Company.
