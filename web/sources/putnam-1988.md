relevance (content): the book whose appendix proves the *triviality* result — that every ordinary
open physical system implements every finite-state automaton under a suitable mapping — which Putnam
turns against the computational functionalism he had earlier helped found. The result is the
radical-relativity limit invoked by [functional-decomposition-relative](../claims/functional-decomposition-relative.md) and the
unrestricted-triviality position denied by [implementation-requires-counterfactual-structure](../claims/implementation-requires-counterfactual-structure.md).
Distinct from [putnam-1967](putnam-1967.md) (multiple realizability) and [putnam-1963](putnam-1963.md)
(behaviourism).

Reference:
- Putnam, H. (1988). *Representation and reality*. MIT Press.
