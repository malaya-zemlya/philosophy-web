The programmatic statement of the free-energy principle and active inference: perception and action
are two directions of one optimization — perception updates internal states to minimize variational
free energy, action changes the world to do the same. Cited in [no-universal-property](../arguments/no-universal-property.md)
as the closest existing formalization of the [consciousness-freewill-duality](../concepts/consciousness-freewill-duality.md): a single
functional minimized in dual directions across the perception–action axis.

Reference: Friston, K. (2010). The free-energy principle: A unified brain theory? *Nature Reviews
Neuroscience*, *11*(2), 127–138. https://doi.org/10.1038/nrn2787
