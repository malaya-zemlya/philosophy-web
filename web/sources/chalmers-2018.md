relevance (content): the modern generalisation of the **paradox of phenomenal judgment**
([paradox-of-phenomenal-judgment](../concepts/paradox-of-phenomenal-judgment.md)). The *meta-problem* is the (topic-neutral) problem of
explaining why we judge and report that there is a hard problem at all — why we produce
"explanatory-gap" talk — using only functional and physical facts. Chalmers stresses that any such
explanation threatens to *debunk* the phenomenal beliefs those reports express, the tension already at
work in the report-scepticism cluster ([report-scepticism-proves-too-much](../arguments/report-scepticism-proves-too-much.md)).

Reference:
- Chalmers, D. J. (2018). The meta-problem of consciousness. *Journal of Consciousness Studies*,
  *25*(9–10), 6–61.
