relevance (content): the source of James's doctrine of *pure experience* and his radical
empiricism — there is one primal stuff of which both knower and known are made, so "consciousness"
names a relation among experiences rather than a separate entity. A historical root of neutral
monism; grounds [neutral-monism](../positions/neutral-monism.md) and [reality-fundamental-stuff-neutral](../claims/reality-fundamental-stuff-neutral.md).
Distinct from [james-1890](james-1890.md), which grounds the combination problem.

Reference:
- James, W. (1904). Does "consciousness" exist? *The Journal of Philosophy, Psychology and
  Scientific Methods, 1*(18), 477–491. (Reprinted in *Essays in radical empiricism*, Longmans,
  Green, 1912)
