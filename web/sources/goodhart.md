relevance (content): the origin of the principle that a measure optimised against ceases to
measure — the general form of the criterion-gaming worry ([criterion-gaming](../concepts/criterion-gaming.md)) that
[rights-criterion-will-be-gamed](../claims/rights-criterion-will-be-gamed.md) applies to rights criteria.

key passages: Goodhart, C.A.E. (1975), "Problems of Monetary Management: The U.K. Experience"
(orig. statement). The now-canonical pithy formulation is Marilyn Strathern's (1997): "When a
measure becomes a target, it ceases to be a good measure."
