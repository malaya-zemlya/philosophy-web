relevance (content): brain-alignment evidence on the visual side. LLM embeddings of scene *captions*
successfully model brain activity evoked by viewing the corresponding natural scenes — capturing the
selectivities of different visual areas well enough that accurate captions can be *reconstructed from brain
activity*, with the match deriving from the LLM's integration of context beyond individual words.
Complements the conceptual brain-alignment of [xu-2025](xu-2025.md) and grounds
[llm-text-yields-human-aligned-representations](../claims/llm-text-yields-human-aligned-representations.md) / [llm-representations-reply](../arguments/llm-representations-reply.md) on the
visual front.

Reference:
- Doerig, A., Kietzmann, T. C., Allen, E., Wu, Y., Naselaris, T., Kay, K., & Charest, I. (2025). High-level visual representations in the human brain are aligned with large language models. *Nature Machine Intelligence*, *7*(8), 1220–1234. https://doi.org/10.1038/s42256-025-01072-0
