relevance (content): develops *priority cosmopsychism* — the cosmos instantiates phenomenal or
protophenomenal properties and its consciousness is ontologically prior to the consciousness of ordinary
individuals — as an alternative to (micro-)panpsychism. Grounds [cosmopsychism](../positions/cosmopsychism.md) and
[cosmos-is-fundamental-subject](../claims/cosmos-is-fundamental-subject.md).

Reference:
- Nagasawa, Y., & Wager, K. (2017). Panpsychism and priority cosmopsychism. In G. Brüntrup & L. Jaskolla (Eds.), *Panpsychism: Contemporary perspectives* (pp. 113–129). Oxford University Press.
