self-knowledge — the mind perceiving its own operations — to which modern higher-order-perception
theories trace their lineage. Cited as historical background in [order-of-representation](../concepts/order-of-representation.md).

Reference:
- Locke, J. (1975). *An essay concerning human understanding* (P. H. Nidditch, Ed.). Clarendon
  Press. (Original work published 1689)
