relevance (content): the contemporary analytic revival of hylomorphism, treating structure (form)
as a basic ontological and explanatory principle and applying it to the mind-body problem. Grounds
the modern revival cited in [hylomorphism](../positions/hylomorphism.md) and [soul-is-form-of-body](../claims/soul-is-form-of-body.md).

Reference:
- Jaworski, W. (2016). *Structure and the metaphysics of mind: How hylomorphism solves the
  mind-body problem*. Oxford University Press.
