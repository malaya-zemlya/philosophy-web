relevance (content): the paper that proposes the **[imitation game](../concepts/turing-test.md)** (the Turing
test) and reframes "Can machines think?" as the operational question whether a machine can converse
indistinguishably from a human. Turing predicts eventual success and answers nine standing objections.
Grounds [turing-test](../concepts/turing-test.md).

Reference:
- Turing, A. M. (1950). Computing machinery and intelligence. *Mind*, *59*(236), 433–460. https://doi.org/10.1093/mind/LIX.236.433
