relevance (content): Goff's trade-book presentation of (Russellian, constitutive) panpsychism for a
general readership — the popular companion to his technical [goff-2017](goff-2017.md). Its central thesis is
exactly the panpsychist core: consciousness is a fundamental and ubiquitous feature of reality. The
title alludes to Galileo's methodological decision to set the qualitative, experiential aspects of
nature outside the scope of physical science, which Goff identifies as the historical root of the
[hard problem](../concepts/hard-problem.md). Grounds the accessible case for [panpsychism](../concepts/panpsychism.md) and
[panpsychism](../positions/panpsychism.md).

Reference:
- Goff, P. (2019). *Galileo's error: Foundations for a new science of consciousness*. Pantheon Books.
