relevance (content): the experimental realization of counterfactual computation (Kwiat's group):
the result of a quantum search algorithm is obtained even though the computer, in the relevant
sense, did not run. Grounds the empirical footing of
[olympia-quantum-counterfactuals](../arguments/olympia-quantum-counterfactuals.md) — "unexercised" computational structure demonstrably
shapes actual outcomes in the laboratory, not merely in thought experiments.

Reference: Hosten, O., Rakher, M. T., Barreiro, J. T., Peters, N. A., & Kwiat, P. G. (2006).
Counterfactual quantum computation through quantum interrogation. *Nature*, *439*(7079), 949–952.
https://doi.org/10.1038/nature04523
