relevance (content): the principal statement of *analytic idealism* — one universal mind whose
dissociations (on the model of dissociative identity disorder) are individual subjects, with the
physical world as the extrinsic appearance of mental processes. Grounds the analytic-idealist
variant of [idealism](../positions/idealism.md) and [reality-is-fundamentally-mental](../claims/reality-is-fundamentally-mental.md).

Reference:
- Kastrup, B. (2019). *The idea of the world: A multi-disciplinary argument for the mental nature
  of reality*. Iff Books.
