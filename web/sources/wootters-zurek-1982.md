relevance (content): the no-cloning theorem — an unknown quantum state cannot be copied. Grounds
the "no trace to unroll" prong of [olympia-quantum-counterfactuals](../arguments/olympia-quantum-counterfactuals.md): a quantum-coherent
run cannot be recorded step by step (measurement destroys the interference) nor copied for later
replay, so the trace log that the Klara→Olympia construction consumes does not exist for such a
computation.

Reference: Wootters, W. K., & Zurek, W. H. (1982). A single quantum cannot be cloned. *Nature*,
*299*(5886), 802–803. https://doi.org/10.1038/299802a0
