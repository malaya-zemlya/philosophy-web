relevance (content): the programmatic statement of *constructor theory* — the proposal that
fundamental physics be reformulated in terms of counterfactuals (which transformations are
*possible* and which *impossible*), with the actual as a derivative special case. Grounds the
inversion pressed in [olympia-quantum-counterfactuals](../arguments/olympia-quantum-counterfactuals.md): where Maudlin's supervenience
premise treats the actual as fundamental and counterfactuals as second-class, a live research
program in physics runs the priority the other way.

Reference: Deutsch, D. (2013). Constructor theory. *Synthese*, *190*(18), 4331–4359.
https://doi.org/10.1007/s11229-013-0279-z
