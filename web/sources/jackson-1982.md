relevance (content): the source of the **Knowledge Argument** ("Mary the colour scientist") against
physicalism. Grounds [knowledge-argument](../concepts/knowledge-argument.md) and the [knowledge-argument](../arguments/knowledge-argument.md) family.

key passages:
- Jackson, Frank (1982), "Epiphenomenal Qualia," *Philosophical Quarterly* 32(127): 127–136 — Mary,
  who knows all the physical facts about colour vision from inside a black-and-white room, and learns
  something on first seeing red. (See also Jackson 1986, "What Mary Didn't Know.")
- Notable: Jackson **later recanted**, coming to reject the argument and accept physicalism (the
  "knowledge" being a new *acquaintance/representation*, not a new fact) — recorded so the web does not
  attribute to him a view he abandoned; the argument is imported on its merits, not as his settled view.
