Argues from experimental psychology that the felt experience of consciously willing an action is a
*construction* — an inference the mind draws when thought precedes action consistently — rather than
a perception of genuine causal origination. The output-side twin of illusionism about phenomenal
consciousness; cited in [zombie-dualizes-to-volitional-zombie](../claims/zombie-dualizes-to-volitional-zombie.md) and
[deflationism-entails-compatibilism](../claims/deflationism-entails-compatibilism.md) as the free-will entry paired with
[illusionism](../positions/illusionism.md) in the duality dictionary.

Reference: Wegner, D. M. (2002). *The illusion of conscious will*. MIT Press.
