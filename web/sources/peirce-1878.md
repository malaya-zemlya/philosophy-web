relevance (content): the canonical statement of the *pragmatic maxim* — that the whole meaning of a
conception lies in its conceivable practical effects, so a difference that makes no difference to
practice is no difference at all. The ancestor of the usefulness axis in
[unobservables-judged-by-coherence-and-usefulness](../claims/unobservables-judged-by-coherence-and-usefulness.md) and the intersubjective standards discussed
in [unobservability-in-principle](../concepts/unobservability-in-principle.md).

Reference:
- Peirce, C. S. (1878). How to make our ideas clear. *Popular Science Monthly, 12*, 286–302.
