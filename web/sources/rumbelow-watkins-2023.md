relevance (content): the discovery of "glitch tokens" in GPT models — anomalous tokens (most famously
` SolidGoldMagikarp`) that entered the byte-pair-encoding vocabulary but were almost absent from the
training corpus, so the model held a name, index, and essentially untrained embedding for them yet
behaved erratically when one was actually used. The real-world instance of the use/mention predicament
dramatised by [llmary](../concepts/llmary.md).

Reference:
- Rumbelow, J., & Watkins, M. (2023, February 5). *SolidGoldMagikarp (plus, prompt generation)*.
  LessWrong. https://www.lesswrong.com/posts/aPeJE8bSo6rAFoLqg/solidgoldmagikarp-plus-prompt-generation
