relevance (content): the canonical statement of the *interventionist* (manipulationist) theory of
causation — X causes Y iff some possible intervention on X would change Y — on which
counterfactual-causal dependencies are objective features of the world rather than artifacts of
description. Grounds the objectivity claim in [counterfactual-structure-objective](../claims/counterfactual-structure-objective.md).

Reference:
- Woodward, J. (2003). *Making things happen: A theory of causal explanation*. Oxford University
  Press.
