relevance (content): the joint volume behind the twentieth-century "Pauli–Jung" dual-aspect view,
on which the mental and the material are complementary aspects of one underlying reality.
Grounds the modern variant of [dual-aspect-monism](../positions/dual-aspect-monism.md) and
[reality-two-aspects-mental-physical](../claims/reality-two-aspects-mental-physical.md).

Reference:
- Jung, C. G., & Pauli, W. (1955). *The interpretation of nature and the psyche* (P. Silz & R. F.
  C. Hull, Trans.). Pantheon Books.
