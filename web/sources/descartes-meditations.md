relevance (content): the classic source of *substance dualism* — Descartes' "Real Distinction" argument
(Meditation VI) that the mind is a thinking, non-extended substance genuinely distinct from the
extended body. The historical anchor for the substance-dualist grade of
[consciousness-fundamental-nonphysical](../claims/consciousness-fundamental-nonphysical.md).

Reference:
- Descartes, R. (1996). *Meditations on first philosophy* (J. Cottingham, Trans.). Cambridge University Press. (Original work published 1641)
