relevance (content): the canonical statement of *eliminative materialism*. Paul Churchland argues
that folk psychology is an empirical theory of behaviour, that it is a stagnant and radically false
one, and that a mature neuroscience will *replace* rather than reduce its posits — so beliefs,
desires, and the rest do not exist. Grounds [folk-psychology-is-false-theory](../claims/folk-psychology-is-false-theory.md) and
[eliminative-materialism](../positions/eliminative-materialism.md).

Reference:
- Churchland, P. M. (1981). Eliminative materialism and the propositional attitudes. *The Journal
  of Philosophy, 78*(2), 67–90. https://doi.org/10.2307/2025900
