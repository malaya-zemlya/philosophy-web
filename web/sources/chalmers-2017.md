relevance (content): Chalmers' taxonomy of the panpsychist family — micro- vs cosmo-, constitutive vs
emergentist, panexperientialist vs panqualityist, and panpsychism vs panprotopsychism — together with
his anatomy of the *combination problem* into the subject-, quality-, and structure-combination
problems. Grounds the varieties section of [panpsychism](../concepts/panpsychism.md) and the
structure of [combination-problem](../arguments/combination-problem.md).

Reference:
- Chalmers, D. J. (2017). Panpsychism and panprotopsychism. In G. Brüntrup & L. Jaskolla (Eds.), *Panpsychism: Contemporary perspectives* (pp. 19–47). Oxford University Press.
