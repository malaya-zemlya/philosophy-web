relevance (content): the "bomb tester" — the canonical demonstration that in quantum mechanics an
object's *unexercised* disposition (a bomb that *would have* absorbed a photon but never does) has
measurable effects on actual outcomes. Grounds the physical premise of
[olympia-quantum-counterfactuals](../arguments/olympia-quantum-counterfactuals.md): the classical picture on which never-triggered
machinery is causally idle is false at the fundamental level.

Reference: Elitzur, A. C., & Vaidman, L. (1993). Quantum mechanical interaction-free measurements.
*Foundations of Physics*, *23*(7), 987–997. https://doi.org/10.1007/BF00736012
