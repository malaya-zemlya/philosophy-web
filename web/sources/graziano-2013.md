relevance (content): the attention schema theory — awareness is the brain's simplified, schematic
model of its own attention, so introspective descriptions are coarse models of the underlying
processing rather than direct read-outs of it. Cited in
[llm-access-similarity-not-encoding](../claims/llm-access-similarity-not-encoding.md) as a precedent for introspection reaching a simplified
model, not the encoding itself.

- Graziano, M. S. A. (2013). *Consciousness and the social brain*. Oxford University Press.
