relevance (content): the paper that names and defends *illusionism* — the view that phenomenal
consciousness does not exist and that introspection misrepresents our states as having intrinsic
qualia, so the hard problem is replaced by the "illusion problem" of explaining why experience
*seems* phenomenal. Grounds [illusionism](../positions/illusionism.md) and [phenomenality-is-illusion](../claims/phenomenality-is-illusion.md).

Reference:
- Frankish, K. (2016). Illusionism as a theory of consciousness. *Journal of Consciousness Studies,
  23*(11–12), 11–39.
