relevance (content): the canonical modern statement of the **causal argument for physicalism** and
of the **completeness of physics** premise it turns on. Grounds
[completeness-of-physics](../concepts/completeness-of-physics.md), [causal-argument-for-physicalism](../arguments/causal-argument-for-physicalism.md),
[physical-domain-complete](../claims/physical-domain-complete.md), and the materialist stance recorded in [physicalism](../positions/physicalism.md).
Papineau's appendix "The History of the Completeness of Physics" is the standard source for the
claim that the closure of the physical is an *empirical* discovery rather than an a priori
assumption.

Reference:
- Papineau, D. (2002). *Thinking about consciousness*. Clarendon Press.
