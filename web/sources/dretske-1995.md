consciousness, identifying the qualitative properties of experience with the external properties
the experience represents, paired with an evolutionary/teleological account of representation.
Grounds the Dretske attribution in [phenomenal-character-is-representational](../claims/phenomenal-character-is-representational.md) and
[representationalism](../positions/representationalism.md).
