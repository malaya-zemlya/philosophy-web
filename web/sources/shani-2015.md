relevance (content): a leading statement of *cosmopsychism* as a holistic alternative to atomistic
panpsychism: an all-pervading cosmic consciousness is the single ontological ultimate, and individual
conscious creatures are grounded in it rather than composing it. Grounds [cosmopsychism](../positions/cosmopsychism.md) and
[cosmos-is-fundamental-subject](../claims/cosmos-is-fundamental-subject.md).

Reference:
- Shani, I. (2015). Cosmopsychism: A holistic approach to the metaphysics of experience. *Philosophical Papers*, *44*(3), 389–437. https://doi.org/10.1080/05568641.2015.1106709
