relevance (content): Goff's systematic defence of (Russellian, constitutive) panpsychism: physicalism
cannot account for consciousness, dualism is unappealing, and panpsychism is the stable middle that
locates experience among the intrinsic natures physics leaves blank. Also a leading treatment of the
*combination problem* as the central obstacle the view must clear. Grounds the case-for section of
[panpsychism](../concepts/panpsychism.md), [panpsychism](../positions/panpsychism.md), and
[combination-problem](../arguments/combination-problem.md).

Reference:
- Goff, P. (2017). *Consciousness and fundamental reality*. Oxford University Press.
