relevance (content): the work that reintroduced *intentionality* into modern philosophy and proposed
that the directedness of mental phenomena upon an object — their "aboutness" — is "the mark of the
mental," the feature distinguishing mental from physical phenomena. Cited in
[philosophy-of-mind](../concepts/philosophy-of-mind.md) (and underwriting [intentionality](../concepts/intentionality.md)) for the intentionality
strand of the field.

Reference:
- Brentano, F. (1995). *Psychology from an empirical standpoint* (A. C. Rancurello, D. B. Terrell, &
  L. L. McAlister, Trans.). Routledge. (Original work published 1874)
