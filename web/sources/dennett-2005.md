relevance (content): the source of the **RoboMary** counter-image — a robot that knows the complete
functional story of its own colour vision can compute and pre-instantiate the total state seeing red
would induce, meeting its first red object with no update. The constructive counter-case used in
[mary-misimagined](../arguments/mary-misimagined.md) and in the hard-line reply [mary-learns-nothing-new](../claims/mary-learns-nothing-new.md). RoboMary
appears in the chapter "What RoboMary Knows." Distinct from [dennett-1991](dennett-1991.md) (*Consciousness
Explained*).

Reference:
- Dennett, D. C. (2005). What RoboMary knows. In *Sweet dreams: Philosophical obstacles to a science of
  consciousness* (pp. 103–130). MIT Press.
