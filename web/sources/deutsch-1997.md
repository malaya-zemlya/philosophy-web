relevance (content): contains Deutsch's argument against solipsism, on which the doctrine, taken seriously,
must reconstruct within the self an entity with exactly the lawful, surprising, autonomous structure that
realism attributes to the external world — so solipsism is realism plus unexplained surplus, a *bad
explanation* to be rejected rather than refuted. Grounds [solipsism-parasitic-on-realism](../claims/solipsism-parasitic-on-realism.md) and
[deutsch-anti-solipsism](../arguments/deutsch-anti-solipsism.md).

Reference:
- Deutsch, D. (1997). *The fabric of reality: The science of parallel universes—and its implications*. Penguin.
