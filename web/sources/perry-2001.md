relevance (content): the primary source of the **indexical / self-locating reply** to the knowledge
argument. Perry, defending "antecedent physicalism", argues that what Mary gains on seeing red is
self-locating knowledge of how she herself reacts to red — genuine, new-to-her facts about her own
situation that no objective description was ever owed. Grounds [indexical-reply](../arguments/indexical-reply.md) and its
conclusion [mary-gains-self-locating-knowledge](../claims/mary-gains-self-locating-knowledge.md).

Reference:
- Perry, J. (2001). *Knowledge, possibility, and consciousness*. MIT Press. (Based on the 1999 Jean
  Nicod Lectures.)
