relevance (content): surveys triviality arguments about computational implementation, arguing they
defeat the bare mapping account and pressing that dispositional/counterfactual fixes face their own
gerrymandering worries. Cited as a denier of [implementation-requires-counterfactual-structure](../claims/implementation-requires-counterfactual-structure.md).
