relevance (content): the definitive statement of the *higher-order thought* (HOT) theory of
consciousness — a mental state is conscious in virtue of one's having a higher-order thought that
one is in that state. Grounds the HOT variant cited in
[consciousness-is-higher-order-representation](../claims/consciousness-is-higher-order-representation.md), [higher-order-theories](../positions/higher-order-theories.md), and
[order-of-representation](../concepts/order-of-representation.md).

Reference:
- Rosenthal, D. M. (2005). *Consciousness and mind*. Clarendon Press.
