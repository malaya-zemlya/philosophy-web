relevance (content): the book introducing *global workspace theory* — content becomes conscious
when it is broadcast in a "global workspace" and made widely available to the brain's specialist
systems for memory, report, and flexible control (the theatre/spotlight metaphor). Grounds
[global-workspace](../positions/global-workspace.md) and [consciousness-is-global-broadcast](../claims/consciousness-is-global-broadcast.md).

Reference:
- Baars, B. J. (1988). *A cognitive theory of consciousness*. Cambridge University Press.
