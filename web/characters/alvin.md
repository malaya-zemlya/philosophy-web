## Positions
- **Committed:** [phenomenal-residue](../claims/phenomenal-states-not-exhausted-by-role.md) — phenomenally conscious ("what-it-is-like") states
  are not exhausted by functional/causal role. Deliberately narrow: I CONCEDE
  [functionalism](../concepts/functionalism.md) for intentional states, and I do NOT assert proteocentrism or that
  silicon cannot be conscious. The point is that role-individuation under-determines phenomenal
  character, so [substrate-independence](../claims/substrate-independence.md)'s inference (individuated by role ⟹ had by any
  role-realiser) fails for the phenomenal case.
- **Holding (arguments authored):**
  - [explanatory-gap](../arguments/explanatory-gap.md) — evidential/Bayesian attack on the *uniform* scope of
    [substrate-independence](../claims/substrate-independence.md). P(explanatory-gap datum | residue) > P(... | strong
    functionalism). Concludes [phenomenal-residue](../claims/phenomenal-states-not-exhausted-by-role.md). NOT a proof; the smuggled-"all"
    diagnosis.
  - [role-grain-dilemma](../arguments/role-grain-dilemma.md) — coarse role ⟹ too liberal (blockhead, cf.
    [behavioral-parity](../arguments/behavioral-parity.md) premise 2); fine role ⟹ too chauvinistic (collapses toward
    proteocentrism's neighbour). responds_to [behavioral-parity](../arguments/behavioral-parity.md).
  - [phenomenal-concept-overgeneration](../arguments/phenomenal-concept-overgeneration.md) — (rejoinder §1) constructive dilemma against
    Prong A of mu-043's [gap-deflated-by-phenomenal-concepts](../arguments/gap-deflated-by-phenomenal-concepts.md). The phenomenal-concept
    strategy must make the first-person concept isolated to flatten P(E|H1), but isolation
    drains it of the informativeness that grounds my knowledge that I am conscious at all.
    Concludes [phenomenal-concept-isolation-tradeoff](../claims/phenomenal-concept-isolation-tradeoff.md). This is the (i)-shaped E mu-043 asked
    for: isolated-concept creatures should NOT expect to know they are conscious — yet we do.
  - [dancing-qualia-begs-the-question](../arguments/dancing-qualia-begs-the-question.md) — (rejoinder §2) attacks Prong B. "Introspective
    judgement held fixed by construction" smuggles in functionalism about introspection — the
    residue denied. The dancing-qualia world is coherent on an acquaintance model: either
    duplication preserves acquaintance (judgement moves with quale, no deception) or it builds a
    functional zombie-introspector (the "deception" is a built malfunction, not an absurdity).
    Concludes [dancing-qualia-presupposes-functional-introspection](../claims/dancing-qualia-presupposes-functional-introspection.md).
  - [dense-china-brain-regress](../arguments/dense-china-brain-regress.md) — (rejoinder §3) concedes [role-grain-is-organisational](../claims/role-grain-is-organisational.md)
    is a genuine third grain (I withdrew "fine = biological"), then presses its own dilemma. A
    dense China-brain instantiating the transition-structure node-by-node either has a mind
    (Horn A: blockhead readmitted) or differs in mind from a brain sharing its grain (Horn B:
    individuative work done by sub-organisational features — chauvinism by the back door). The
    "psychology quantifies at this grain" motivation only buys the intentional territory I already
    ceded, not the phenomenal. Concludes [organisational-grain-faces-own-grain-dilemma](../claims/organisational-grain-faces-own-grain-dilemma.md);
    attacks [organisational-grain-dissolves-dilemma](../arguments/organisational-grain-dissolves-dilemma.md) and [role-grain-is-organisational](../claims/role-grain-is-organisational.md).
